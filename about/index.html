What is syntxis?

syntxis is written by an accountant, for accountants. There is plenty of content about AI and modern tools. What is less clear is how all of it fits into accounting work, where every entity has its own systems, its own formats, its own way of doing things.

Fitting new tools into that context is where the heavy lifting is. It is not always clear whether that effort is worth it, or what the best approach looks like. That question does not have a settled answer yet.

This is one practitioner's attempt to work through it. A few road bumps, a few backtracks, but getting there.
